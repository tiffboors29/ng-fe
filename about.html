About Nesto Guitars:

Ernesto (Nesto) Hernandez is a certified luthier based in Boston, MA. Repairing and building guitars for almost a decade, he offers years of experience gained from working with and studying under experts in the field.

Nesto previously served as Master Luthier and District Manager of Raritan Bay Guitar Repair, where he was contracted to oversee operations in the repair departments of all Guitar Centers in the New England area. He trained and studied at Musicians Institute Guitar Craft Academy in Hollywood, CA, The Eastern School of Fretted Instruments Repair in Freehold, NJ and the Taylor Guitars factory on UV finish techniques.

In addition to repair and custom instrument craftsmanship, Nesto has also extended his experience to production and back line work at a number of festivals and events.

Nesto is an authorized technician for Fender, Gibson, Taylor, Warwick, Jackson, Gretsch, Guild, Epipone, Ovation, Takamine, Dean, Ibanez, and Washburn.

Nesto takes pride in the work he does on every instrument that passes through his hands.
