Alder Body Bass

This custom bass was handmade without CNC machines. It is crafted with an ultra light alder body and a single piece flame maple top. The neck is 7 ply maple blackwalnut with an ebony fretboard and bone nut black Gotoh steel bridge. Using only the best quality tone woods and hardware for this instrument, the bass features an Aguilar OBP3 preamp, Bartolini jazz bass and soap bar pickups, and Dunlop flush-strap locks.




Custom Greenburst Super Strat

This roasted neck super strat is custom finished with a green burst. Fitted with Seymour Duncan Alnico 2 pickups, chrome Gotoh hardware and locking tuning keys, this cutom built guitar features the best hardware for the best look and sound.




Purple Haze Fender

Starting as a cream Fender Jazz Master, this guitar was custom painted with Paint Huffer metal flake.
